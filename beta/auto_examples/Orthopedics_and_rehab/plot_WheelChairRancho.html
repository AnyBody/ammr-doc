Wheel Chair model¶

A model of a person sitting in a wheelchair, the model is driven by motion capture data.

Main file: Application/Examples/WheelChairRancho/WheelChairRancho.main.any

This is a model of a person sitting in a wheelchair. The model only comprises the upper right arm.

The model is driven using motion capture data from kindly provided by:

Philip S. Requejo Ph.D. Pathokinesiology Laboratory Rancho Los Amigos National Rehabilitation Center

The force between the hand and the rim is also based on measurements.
