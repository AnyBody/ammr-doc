The Symmetric Mandible

The Symmetric Mandible#

This mandible model is based on CT scans of a ”normal” face of a 30-year-old male. It contains 24 hill-type muscles and 4 DoF. It was developed by Dr. Mark de Zee at Aarhus University, Denmark and published and presented at several scientific meetings. [1]
