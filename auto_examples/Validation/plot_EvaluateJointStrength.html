Evaluate joint strength¶

This model shows how to enable the built-in studies to evaluate joint strength. The studies can be enabled by setting the switch: #define EVALUATE_JOINT_STRENGTH 1

The studies will appear in the HumanModel.EvaluateJointStrength folder.

There are currently only joint strength studies for the arm model and the Leg models.

Note

This will include a lot of studies (two per DOF) so they will affect perfomance when loading and switching between tabs/operations.

See also
