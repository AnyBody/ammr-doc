Application Examples¶

In the repository, you can find musculoskeletal applications from a wide area of interests. These models are from various AnyBody users, and all demonstrate features from the AnyBody Modeling System.

Note

All examples are powerful computational models where variables of interest have been biomechanically checked to make sense. However, when modifying or using models for other purposes results may occur that cannot be interpreted by anatomical or physiological considerations.
